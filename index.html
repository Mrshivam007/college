Our Introduction

Chhatrapati Shivaji Institute of Technology is a premier technical college in the state of Chhattisgarh with a very bright performance in the field of engineering education for about past two decades. CSIT is an AICTE recognized, ISO 9001 - 2015 Certified, and has been accredited to NAAC & NBA. It is affiliated to Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekanand Technical University, Bhilai. CSIT has achieved commendable ranks at national level from time to time. It is largely known for its academic excellence, R&D activities and dedication towards overall grooming of its students. Since its inception, the Institute has progressed in leaps and bounds, and is undergoing a continuous process of improvement in all aspects of curricular and extracurricular activities. One can experience a polarization of facilities and resources of metro setting as well as peace, environment and true nature of the rural setting on this campus. The Institute is located on Durg-Balod, Dallirajhara Road at Shivaji Nagar near Kolihapuri, Dist. Durg (C.G.), 4 Kms away from the Collectorate and 6 Kms from Railway Station, Durg. The grandeur of the location provides a unique environment for education in close proximity with nature which is farfetched from the hum-drum of the city.
